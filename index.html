Silviu S. Craciunas

 I am no longer at the department; since October 2011 I am working at TTTech Computertechnik AG, Vienna, Austria, mostly doing research in the field of deterministic networks. I will try to keep the website up to date in terms of publications; nevertheless, some of the information may be outdated.
