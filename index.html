Silviu S. Craciunas

Since 2025, I have been employed at NXP Semiconductors as a Senior Principal Scientist, working primarily in the automotive domain. I also hold a part-time Associate Professor position in the Department of Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at the Technical University of Denmark, where my research focuses on Time-Sensitive Networking (TSN), edge AI, and safety-critical systems. Prior to these roles, I held research positions at TTTech Computertechnik AG and TTTech Auto AG in Vienna, Austria. My work there focused on deterministic networking and real-time systems across industrial, aerospace, and automotive domains.
