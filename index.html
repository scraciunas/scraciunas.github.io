Silviu S. Craciunas

  Since 2011, I have been working at TTTech Computertechnik AG and TTTech Auto AG, Vienna, Austria, primarily doing research in the field of deterministic networks and real-time systems. I will try to keep the website up to date; nevertheless, some of the information may be outdated.

Research interests
  • Advanced scheduling and synthesis methods for safety-critical and predictable distributed systems
  • Time-sensitive networks, Deterministic (Time-triggered) Ethernet
  • Operating systems, Virtualization/Containerization, Dynamic scheduling algorithms, Power-aware scheduling
  • Real-time and safety-critical embedded systems, Cyber-physical systems
  • Cloud computing, Internet of Things, Autonomous systems and vehicles
